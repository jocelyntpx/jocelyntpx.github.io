More often than not, many clubs and faculties use their Instagram page as their only platform for their merchandise sales. But other than your own faculty’s Instagram page, who else do you follow? Thus, we often miss out on some really nice NUS t-shirts that we want to buy but have no idea which committee or faculty sells it! Hence, you start stalking all the halls and faculty’s instagram page but to no avail… You start thinking to yourself why can’t there be a central website where everyone can put up their merchandise sales so that everyone has perfect information about the available merchandise and do not have to rely on connections just to get a nice NUS shirt.

As such, we hope that this website will be able to make it easier and more convenient for NUS students to support NUS fundraisers when getting NUS merchandise.
