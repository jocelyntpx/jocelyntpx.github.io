About

A summer project by 2 NUS students with the idea of increasing the convenience for NUS students to support NUS fundraisers when getting NUS merchandise.

More often than not, many clubs and faculties use their Instagram page as their only platform for their merchandise sales. However, not all Instagram pages are followed by students. Thus, students often miss out on some really nice NUS merchandise as they have no idea which committee is selling it. Fundraisers may also lack the opportunity to reach out to more audience. Hence, with a centralised website where all NUS students can put up their merchandise sales, everyone has perfect information about the all available merchandize.
